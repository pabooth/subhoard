# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming, safe, and respectful environment
for everyone participating in this project, regardless of background,
identity, experience, or level of technical expertise.

## Expected behaviour

Project participants should:

- communicate respectfully and constructively;
- welcome questions, feedback, and differing viewpoints;
- focus criticism on ideas and code rather than people;
- accept responsibility for mistakes and correct them;
- respect privacy, consent, and the project's security boundaries.

## Unacceptable behaviour

The following behaviour is not acceptable:

- harassment, discrimination, intimidation, or personal attacks;
- sexualized language, imagery, or unwelcome attention;
- threats, stalking, doxxing, or disclosure of private information;
- deliberate disruption, trolling, or sustained bad-faith participation;
- publishing credentials, private content, or security details without
  authorization.

## Scope

This code applies to project repositories, issues, pull requests, reviews,
discussions, and other spaces where someone is representing the project.

## Reporting and enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainer using the contact methods
listed on the [repository owner's GitHub profile](https://github.com/pabooth).
Do not open a public issue containing personal or sensitive details.

For abuse occurring on GitHub itself, you may also contact
[GitHub Support](https://support.github.com/contact/report-abuse).

Reports will be reviewed as promptly and impartially as possible. Maintainers
may edit or remove contributions, issue a warning, temporarily restrict
participation, or permanently ban a participant when necessary. The privacy
and safety of reporters will be respected.
